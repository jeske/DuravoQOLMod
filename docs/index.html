Smart Minion Pathfinding

Your minions now intelligently navigate around terrain. When blocked by obstacles, they find paths around them instead of getting stuck. When truly isolated, they phase back to you to prevent cheese strategies.

Persistent Position

Your character stays exactly where you left them when you log out. No more spawning at the world origin just because you want to take a break for dinner, and no more logout escapes in medium and hardcore.

Reactive Armor Shield

Low Tier chestpiece reactive shield effect makes early crafts feel exciting and gives visual feedback that helps new players learn combat.

Shiny Ore Sparkle

New 2pc crafted armor set bonuses allow mix-and-match across tiers. Shiny 2pc bonus causes ores just out of torch range to sparkle, making early mining more fun.

Crafting Info Panel

Organized panel for craftable item recipies, and tootips. Remembers seen items across all your characters and worlds.
